About HD CUTZ

HD CUTZ AL THE BARBER delivers elite grooming in the heart of Narok. Located at Kim's Breeze Plaza, we offer precision fades, sharp line-ups, beard sculpting and premium treatments. Walk-ins welcome — appointments recommended for weekends.

Our skilled barbers use top-quality tools and products to ensure every cut is perfect. From classic styles to modern trends, we cater to all ages and preferences. Experience the difference at HD CUTZ!
